"Wanted" Poster for Armed Pillagers

TL;DR: suggestion to implement wanted posters for pillager mob in villages (w/ pictures)

 

Minecraft's villager mob has gained a lot of features and usability in the game since its release in 2011. Villagers now come in a variety of attire based on their native biome and job/hobby in addition to other cool features. Counter to their existence, the pillager mob was recently introduced to the game as an enemy. Pillagers look fairly similar to villagers except for their attire and grey-ish skin tone (see below).

While pillagers get added to the list of yet another hostile mob to the player, they are also dangerous to villagers. Pillagers conduct "raids", which are spontaneous village attacks often triggered by recent player activity within or near the village who possess the "Bad Omen" effect. Pillagers, like zombie villagers, are harmful to the villager and can kill.

 

Suggested Feature: Wanted Poster?

 

[pic poster]

description . . .

 

To give the villages a more detailed look, why not include wanted banners? Theoretically these signs would be posted on bulletins and the sides of villager homes. Currently villages contain bells that can be rung to warn all villagers to safely return to their home. But aesthetically speaking, adding a banner feature would be a cool idea. Simple enough, it could be implemented as a picture!
