Welcome to My Memoji Network!

1 NOV 2020    •    tags:

I was looking online for a graphical way to visually represent all the connections I made in the CS, ITI, and Engineering communities but there wasn't a good, simple way to do it. So, what you're looking at is a network graph I created using Apple's trademark memoji stickers and the Vis.js javascript library!

It's not perfect and there are a lot of things that I'll be working on with this to figure out the easiest way to display nodes in the least crowded or confusing way. Coming soon: clickable nodes that have a person's professional information (i.e. linkedin, website, etc;)
